Introduction

The Health Companion Super App is designed to help individuals manage their medications and doctor appointments efficiently. This case study explores the UX/UI design process behind its development.

User Personas

Our target users include:

  • Sarah Thompson: A busy professional who frequently forgets to take medication.
  • Robert Williams: An elderly patient who needs voice reminders and a simple UI.
  • Dr. Emily Foster: A healthcare provider who wants real-time patient adherence tracking.

User Journey

Users interact with the app in the following way:

  1. Onboarding: Users set up their medication and appointment schedules.
  2. Daily Reminders: Smart notifications help them stay on track.
  3. Medication Tracking: Users log their doses and receive insights.
  4. Appointment Scheduling: The app simplifies booking and reminders.
  5. Health Insights: Users receive reports on their adherence progress.
